# 建築性能 (Building Performance)

## Skills

* [綠建築標章](綠建築標章/green-building-label/SKILL.md) - Applying for Taiwan Green Building Label (EEWH) or Candidate Green Building Certificate
* [智慧建築](智慧建築/smart-building-label/SKILL.md) - Applying for Taiwan Smart Building Label or designing intelligent building systems
* [建築能效](建築能效/building-energy-rating/SKILL.md) - Applying for Taiwan Building Energy Efficiency Label or calculating building energy performance
* [低碳建築](低碳建築/low-carbon-buildings/SKILL.md) - Applying for Taiwan Low Embodied Carbon Building Label (LEBR) or calculating embodied carbon
* [健康建築](健康建築/well-building/SKILL.md) - Designing for WELL Building Certification or improving indoor health environment
* [永續建築](永續建築/building-sustainability/SKILL.md) - Designing green building and sustainable architecture, integrating environmental assessment, energy efficiency, and resource circularity
* [LEED](LEED/leed/SKILL.md) - Applying for LEED certification or designing green buildings according to USGBC standards
* [光環境設計](光環境設計/daylighting-design/SKILL.md) - Designing and analyzing daylighting systems, optimizing natural light utilization, and evaluating visual comfort
* [室內環境](室內環境/indoor-environment/SKILL.md) - Designing indoor environmental quality including daylighting, ventilation, acoustic comfort, and air quality
* [音環境設計](音環境設計/acoustic-design/SKILL.md) - Designing architectural acoustics, including room acoustics, noise control, and sound insulation
* [計算工具](計算工具/architect-calculator/SKILL.md) - Performing architect calculations including floor area analysis, structural estimation, MEP load calculation, and cost estimation
